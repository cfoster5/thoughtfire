Creative Problem Solving Inventory

This inventory is designed to describe your method of problem solving. The aim of the inventory is to describe how you problem solve, not to evaluate your problem solving ability. You may find it hard to choose the words that best describe your problem solving style because there are no right or wrong answers. Different characteristics are equally good.

Assign numbers from 4 - 1 to the words that characterize your problem solving style.
